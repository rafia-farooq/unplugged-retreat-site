Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

When you register enter your location and we will arrange bus tickets for to you to get to the Unplugged retreat.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Everyone is welcome! We offer different plans depending on how long you plan to stay.

What size teams can you accommodate?

Our activities can easily fit in a team of 30 people. We have extra rooms available for larger teams. We say: The bigger the better.

Is there really no wifi available?

We encourage that you stay disconnected to the outside world while your stay here but we have a designated room for absolute necessary wifi usage.
